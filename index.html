How to Reduce Consumption for Each Indicator

⚡ Electricitat

  • Install presence sensors to automatically switch off lighting
  • Replace all lighting with low-consumption LED (−50% lighting)
  • Install photovoltaic solar panels on the roof (−40% bill)
  • Schedule automatic shutdown of electronic equipment outside school hours
  • Review maintenance of air conditioning units and boilers every year
  • Implement intelligent energy management (Building Management System)

💧 Aigua

  • Install aerators on all taps (reduces flow by up to 50%)
  • Replace conventional cisterns with dual-flush cisterns
  • Collect rainwater for irrigation of landscaped areas
  • Detect and repair leaks periodically with digital meters
  • Raise student awareness with campaigns on responsible use

📝 Office Supplies

  • "Zero Paper" policy for internal communications and administrative documents
  • Digitise all academic and administrative procedures in the cloud
  • Use refillable pens and markers instead of single-use ones
  • Double-sided printing by default on all printers
  • Reuse paper printed on one side as drafts
  • Purchase consumables with certified eco-label

🧼 Cleaning Products

  • Replace paper towels with low-consumption air dryers
  • Purchase soaps and products in bulk in reusable containers
  • Use exclusively certified probiotic and ecological cleaning products
  • Eliminate single-use plastic containers in all spaces
  • Evaluate environmental impact when selecting cleaning suppliers
